## 2.2.7
- 不放回抽樣，與順序無關，使用組合方法（Combination）
- 共 140 個樣本，10 個不合格，130 個合格

- a. How many different samples are possible?
  1. 求所有可能的組合數
  2. 140 chips 中選 5 chips 的組合數
  3. $C^{140}_{5}=\underline{416,965,528\ \blacksquare}$
- b. How many samples contain exactly one nonconforming chip?
  1. 求恰好 1 個不合格的組合數
  2. 10 個不合格 chips 中選 1 個，130 個合格 chips 中選 4 個
  3. $C^{10}_{1} \times C^{130}_{4} = \underline{113,588,800\ \blacksquare}$
- c. How many samples contain at least one nonconforming chip?
  1. 求至少 1 個不合格的組合數
  2. 總組合數 - 全合格 chips 的組合數
  3. $C^{140}_{5} - C^{130}_{5} = 416965528 - 286243776 = \underline{130,721,752\ \blacksquare}$

## 2.2.14
- 不放回抽樣，與順序無關，使用組合方法（Combination）
- 共 12 個樣本，2 個不合格，10 個合格

- a. How many samples contain exactly 1 nonconforming part?
  1. 求恰好 1 個不合格的組合數
  2. 2 個不合格中選 1 個，10 個合格中選 2 個
  3. $C^{2}_{1} \times C^{10}_{2} = \underline{90\ \blacksquare}$
- b. How many samples contain at least 1 nonconforming part?
  1. 求至少 1 個不合格的組合數
  2. 有不合格的組合數 = 總組合數 - 全合格的組合數
  3. $C^{12}_{3} - C^{10}_{3} = 220 - 120 = \underline{100\ \blacksquare}$

## 2.2.15
- 有順序，使用排列方法（Permutation）
- 共 12 個樣本，3 個 knee (K)、4 個 hip (H)、5 個 shoulder (S)
- 意即：{KKKHHHHSSSSS}

- a. How many different sequences are possible?
  1. 求所有可能的排列數
  2. 相同類型的手術彼此不區分，多重排列
  3. $\cfrac{\text{全排列數}}{\text{重複排列數}}=\frac{12!}{3!4!5!}=\underline{27,720\ \blacksquare}$
- b. How many different sequences have all hip, knee, and shoulder surgeries scheduled consecutively?
  1. 求所有同類連續下的排列數
  2. 共 K, H, S 三類，將其視為三個區塊，而區塊內元素皆為同類（只有 1 種排列）。
  3. $3!=\underline{6\ \blacksquare}$
- c. How many different schedules begin and end with a knee surgery?
  1. 求以 K 開始與結束的排列數
  2. 首固定為 K，尾固定為 S，中間排列 10 個
  3. 所以中間的 10 個元素中，有 1 個 K、4 個 H，5 個 S
  4. $\cfrac{\text{全排列數}}{\text{重複排列數}}=\frac{10!}{1!4!5!}=\underline{1,260\ \blacksquare}$

## 2.4.8
- 4 colors ( C), 3 font types (T), 5 font sizes (S), 3 images (I), 5 text phrases (P)
- $A$ 指 C 為 red；$B$ 指 S 不是最小的
- 所有可能的組合數：$4 \times 3 \times 5 \times 3 \times 5 = 900$
- $P(A)=\frac{1 \times 3 \times 5 \times 3 \times 5}{900}=0.25$
- $P(B)=\frac{4 \times 3 \times 4 \times 3 \times 5}{900}=0.8$
- $P(A \cap B)=\frac{1 \times 3 \times 4 \times 3 \times 5}{900}=0.2$

- a. $P(A \cup B)$
  1. A 和 B 事件聯集的機率
  2. 算法 1:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A \cup B)&=P(A)+P(B)-P(A \cap B)\\
      &=0.25+0.8-0.2\\
      &=\underline{0.85\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
  3. 算法 2:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A \cup B)&=P(A)+P(B)-P(A \cap B)\\
      &=\frac{(1\times 3 \times 5 \times 3 \times 5)+(4 \times 3 \times 4 \times 3 \times 5)-(1 \times 3 \times 4 \times 3 \times 5)}{900}\\
      &=\underline{0.85\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
- b. $P(A \cup B')$
  1. A 和 B' 事件聯集的機率
  2. 算法 1:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A \cup B')&=P(A)+P(B')-P(A \cap B')\\
      &=P(A)+1-P(B)-P(A)P(B')\\
      &=0.25+(1-0.8)-(0.25 \times(1-0.8))\\
      &=\underline{0.4\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
  3. 算法 2:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A \cup B')&=P(A)+P(B')-P(A \cap B')\\
      &=0.25 + (1-0.8) - \frac{1 \times 3 \times 1 \times 3 \times 5}{900}\\
      &=\underline{0.4\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
  4. 算法 2:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A \cup B')&=P(A)+P(B')-P(A \cap B')\\
      &=\frac{(1\times 3 \times 5 \times 3 \times 5)+(4 \times 3 \times 1 \times 3 \times 5)-(1 \times 3 \times 1 \times 3 \times 5)}{900}\\
      &=\underline{0.4\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
- c. $P(A' \cup B')$
  1. A' 和 B' 事件聯集的機率
  2. 算法 1:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A' \cup B')&=1-P(A \cap B)\\
      &=1-0.2=\underline{0.8\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
  3. 算法 2:  
      $\begin{aligned}
      P(A' \cup B')&=P(A')+P(B')-P(A' \cap B')\\
      &=(1-P(A))+(1-P(B))-(P(A')P(B'))\\
      &=(1-0.25)+(1-0.8)-((1-0.25)(1-0.8))\\
      &=\underline{0.8\ \blacksquare}\end{aligned}$
  4. 算法 3:

## 2.5.1
- 共 300 個樣本，243 個完成 color transformation (A)、256 個完成 textural transformation (B)

  2. $P(A|B')=\cfrac{P(A \cap B')}{P(B')}$
  3. $\frac{26}{26+18}=\underline{0.59090909090909\ \blacksquare}$

## 2.5.6
- 共 500 個樣本，5 個 defective、495 個 acceptable。
- 每次隨機選 2 個樣本，不放回抽樣，與順序無關，使用組合方法（Combination）

- a. What is the probability that the second one selected is defective given that the first one was defective?
  1. 求第一個 defective 後，第二個 defective 的機率：$P(\text{defective 2nd}|\text{defective 1st})$
  2. 算法 1:
     1. 第一個 defective 後，剩下 4 個 defective，499 個樣本。
     2. $\frac{4}{499}=\underline{0.0080160320641283\ \blacksquare}$
  3. 算法 2:
     1. $P(\text{defective 2nd}|\text{defective 1st})=\cfrac{P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd})}{P(\text{defective 1st})}$
     2. $\frac{\frac{5}{500} \times \frac{4}{499}}{\frac{5}{500}}=\underline{0.0080160320641283\ \blacksquare}$
- b. What is the probability that both are defective?
  1. 求兩個 defective 的機率：$P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd})$
  2. $P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd})=$P(\text{defective 1st})\times P(\text{defective 2nd})$
  3. $\frac{5}{500} \times \frac{4}{499}=\underline{0.000080160320641283\ \blacksquare}$
- c. What is the probability that both are acceptable?
  1. 求兩個 acceptable 的機率：$P(\text{acceptable 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd})$
  2. $P(\text{acceptable 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd})=$P(\text{acceptable 1st})\times P(\text{acceptable 2nd})
  3. 第一個 acceptable 後，剩下 494 個 acceptable，498 個樣本。
  4. $\frac{495}{500} \times \frac{494}{499}=\underline{0.98008016032064\ \blacksquare}$
- d. What is the probability that the third one selected is defective given that the first and second ones selected were defective?
  1. 求第一個和第二個 defective 後，第三個 defective 的機率：$P(\text{defective 3rd}|(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd}))$
  2. 算法 1:
     1. 當第一個和第二個 defective 後，剩下 3 個 defective，498 個樣本。
     2. $\frac{3}{498}=\underline{0.0060240963855422\ \blacksquare}$
  3. 算法 2:  
     $\begin{aligned}P(\text{defective 3rd}|(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd}))&=\frac{P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd} \cap \text{defective 3rd})}{P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd})}\\&=\frac{P(\text{defective 1st}) \times P(\text{defective 2nd}) \times P(\text{defective 3rd})}{P(\text{defective 1st}) \times P(\text{defective 2nd})}\\&=P(\text{defective 3rd})\end{aligned}$
     1. 當第一個和第二個 defective 後，剩下 3 個 defective，498 個樣本。
     2. $P(\text{defective 3rd})=\frac{3}{498}=\underline{0.0060240963855422\ \blacksquare}$
- e. What is the probability that the third one selected is defective given that the first one selected was defective and the second one selected was okay?
  1. 求第一個 defective 和第二個 acceptable 後，第三個 defective 的機率：$P(\text{defective 3rd}|(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd}))$
  2. 算法 1:
     1. 當第一個 defective 和第二個 acceptable 後，剩下 4 個 defective，498 個樣本。
     2. $\frac{4}{498}=\underline{0.0080321285140562\ \blacksquare}$
  3. 算法 2:  
     $\begin{aligned}P(\text{defective 3rd}|(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd}))&=\frac{P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd} \cap \text{defective 3rd})}{P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{acceptable 2nd})}\\&=\frac{P(\text{defective 1st}) \times P(\text{acceptable 2nd}) \times P(\text{defective 3rd})}{P(\text{defective 1st}) \times P(\text{acceptable 2nd})}\\&=P(\text{defective 3rd})\end{aligned}$
     1. 當第一個 defective 和第二個 acceptable 後，剩下 4 個 defective，498 個樣本。
     2. $P(\text{defective 3rd})=\frac{4}{498}=\underline{0.0080321285140562\ \blacksquare}$
- f. What is the probability that all three are defective?
  1. 求三個 defective 的機率：$P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd} \cap \text{defective 3rd})$
  2. $P(\text{defective 1st} \cap \text{defective 2nd} \cap \text{defective 3rd})=P(\text{defective 1st}) \times P(\text{defective 2nd}) \times P(\text{defective 3rd})$
  3. $\frac{5}{500} \times \frac{4}{499} \times \frac{3}{498}=\underline{4.8289349783906\times10^{−7}\ \blacksquare}$

## 2.7.8
- 已知，有 4 個對手（假設分別為 a, b, c, d），每個對手 $P(\text{defeat})=0.8$，且為獨立事件 (independent)。但是，若玩家被打敗（$P(\text{not defeat})=0.2$），則遊戲結束。

- a. What is the probability that a player defeats all four opponents in a game?
  1. 求玩家 defeat 所有 4 個對手的機率：$P(\text{defeat a}\cap\text{defeat b}\cap\text{defeat c}\cap\text{defeat d})$
  2. 由於為獨立事件：  
     $P(\text{defeat a}\cap\text{defeat b}\cap\text{defeat c}\cap\text{defeat d})=P(\text{defeat a})\times P(\text{defeat b})\times P(\text{defeat c})\times P(\text{defeat d})$
  3. $0.8 \times 0.8 \times 0.8 \times 0.8=\underline{0.4096\ \blacksquare}$
- b. What is the probability that a player defeats at least two opponents in a game?
  1. 求玩家 defeat 至少 2 個對手的機率：$P(\text{defeat at least 2})$
  2. 算法 1:
     1. $P(\text{defeat at least 2})=1-P(\text{defeat 0})-P(\text{defeat 1})$
     2. $P(\text{defeat 0})=0.2$ （第一局就被打敗）
     3. $P(\text{defeat 1})=0.8 \times 0.2 = 0.16$ （第一局勝利，第二局被打敗）
     4. $1-0.2-0.16=\underline{0.64\ \blacksquare}$
  3. 算法 2:
     1. $P(\text{defeat at least 2})=P(\text{defeat 2})+P(\text{defeat 3})+P(\text{defeat 4})$
     2. $P(\text{defeat 2})=0.8 \times 0.8 \times 0.2=0.128$

## 錯誤類型

|                          決策                           |        $H_0$ 為真 (True)        |           $H_0$ 為假 (False)           |
| :-----------------------------------------------------: | :-----------------------------: | :------------------------------------: |
| $H_0$ 無法拒絕 (Fail to Reject)/接受 (Accept)<br>(True) | 正確 (Correct)<br> (1-$\alpha$) |        Type II error ($\beta$)         |
|             $H_0$ 拒絕 (Reject)<br>(False)              |     Type I error ($\alpha$)     | 正確 (Correct)<br> (1-$\beta$ = Power) |

### 計算

1.  - 當:
      - $H_0:\ \mu = \mu_0$ (虛無假設)
      - $H_1:\ \mu < \mu_0$ (對立假設)
    - 則:
      - Type I error ($\alpha$) = $P(\mathrm{Reject\ H_0} | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ True})$  
        $\alpha = P(\overline{X} < \mu_0 | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ True}) = P(\overline{X} < \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_0)$
      - Type II error ($\beta$) = $P(\mathrm{Accept\ H_0} | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ False})$  
        $\beta = P(\overline{X} \ge \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_{true}) = P(\overline{X} \ge \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_{true})$
2.  - 當:
      - $H_0:\ \mu = \mu_0$ (虛無假設)
      - $H_1:\ \mu > \mu_0$ (對立假設)
    - 則:
      - Type I error ($\alpha$) = $P(\mathrm{Reject\ H_0} | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ True})$  
        $\alpha = P(\overline{X} > \mu_0 | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ True}) = P(\overline{X} > \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_0)$
      - Type II error ($\beta$) = $P(\mathrm{Accept\ H_0} | \mathrm{H_0\ is\ False})$  
        $\beta = P(\overline{X} \le \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_{true}) = P(\overline{X} \le \mu_0 | \mu = \mu_{true})$
